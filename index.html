WHAT IS ANIMAL RESCUE?

Animal rescue involves the safe removal of animals from situations where they are endangered, neglected, abandoned, or abused, providing them with the necessary care, rehabilitation, and ultimately, new homes. This vital work is typically carried out by animal rescue organizations, shelters, and dedicated volunteers who are committed to improving the lives of these animals. The need for animal rescue arises from various circumstances, including natural disasters, hoarding situations, abandonment, and instances of neglect or abuse. Many animals are left homeless due to their owners' inability to care for them or because they were born as strays. Without intervention, these animals face significant risks, such as malnutrition, disease, and even death.
